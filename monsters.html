chort

Beastly monsters resembling a cross between a bull and a demon. They have powerful horns, sharp claws, and immense strength. Chorts are territorial and aggressive, often found in forests or remote areas.

werewolf

Humans cursed to transform into wolf-like creatures under the full moon. Werewolves are driven by a primal hunger and rage but retain some of their human memories and emotions, making them tragic figures in some stories.

golem

Artificial creatures made of clay, stone, or other materials. They are created through magical means to serve as guardians. Golems are nearly indestructible, emotionless, and follow commands without question.

arachas

Large, spider-like monsters with armored exoskeletons. They are cunning predators that ambush their prey, often hiding in forests or caves.

bruxa

Vampire-like creatures that resemble beautiful women. They are highly intelligent, possess a piercing scream as a weapon, and can move with incredible speed. Bruxae are dangerous due to their seductive and manipulative nature.

leshen

Forest spirits with antlered, skeletal appearances. Leshens are protectors of nature and can summon animals like wolves or ravens to fight for them. They are elusive and hostile to those who harm their domain.

wyvern

Dragon-like creatures with bat-like wings and venomous tails. They are smaller and less intelligent than true dragons but are still formidable predators, often nesting in high places or ruins.
