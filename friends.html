Geralt of Rivia

A professional monster hunter and the series' protagonist. Known as the "White Wolf," he is a skilled swordsman and alchemist, with white hair and cat-like eyes due to Witcher mutations. Geralt is stoic, pragmatic, and deeply moral beneath his gruff exterior.

Yennefer of Vengerberg

A powerful sorceress and Geralt's love interest. She is beautiful, cunning, and ambitious, with striking violet eyes and raven-black hair. Despite her tough demeanor, Yennefer has a compassionate side and a longing for motherhood.

Mousesack (Ermion)

A druid and trusted friend of Geralt and Ciri. Wise and good-natured, he serves as an advisor to Queen Calanthe of Cintra. Mousesack is deeply connected to nature and wields considerable magical power.

Triss Merigold

A skilled sorceress and close friend of Yennefer and Geralt. She is known for her fiery red hair and kindhearted nature. Triss cares deeply for Ciri and plays a protective role in her life, though she struggles with her feelings for Geralt.

Vesemir

The oldest and most experienced Witcher, serving as a mentor and father figure to Geralt and other Witchers. He resides at Kaer Morhen and is wise, patient, and deeply committed to the Witcher way of life.

Eskel

A fellow Witcher and one of Geralt's closest friends. He is calm, dependable, and loyal, often acting as a steadying presence among his peers. Eskel bears a prominent facial scar from his monster-hunting days.

Jaskier (Dandelion)

A flamboyant bard and Geralt's best friend. Known for his wit, charm, and love of music, Jaskier often provides comic relief but is also fiercely loyal. His ballads immortalize Geralts exploits (sometimes with a little embellishment).
